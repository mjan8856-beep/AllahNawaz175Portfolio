FAQ

Common Questions

What does your design process look like start to finish?
Every project starts with a discovery session to understand your business goals, users, and constraints. From there I move through research, information architecture, wireframing, high-fidelity UI, prototyping, and usability testing before a clean developer handoff. The full 16-step framework is in the Process section above.
How long does a typical project take?
A UX Audit typically takes 1–2 weeks. A full Product Redesign ranges from 4–8 weeks depending on scope. Retainer partnerships run on 2-week sprint cycles.
Do you work with international clients?
Absolutely. I'm based in Islamabad, Pakistan and work with clients globally across the US, UK, Europe, and beyond. All collaboration happens remotely via Figma, Slack, and video calls.
What tools do you use for design and developer handoff?
Figma for UI design, prototyping, and design systems. Framer for motion prototypes, Spline 3D for 3D, Maze and UserTesting for research, Adobe Creative Suite for graphic work. Handoffs are delivered with full Figma Inspect documentation and design tokens.
What makes you different from other product designers?
I operate at the intersection of product strategy and visual craft. I think about business outcomes, user psychology, and engineering constraints simultaneously. My work is data-informed and I communicate decisions clearly so teams move faster. Clients often describe me as someone who makes the complex feel simple.
