FAQs.

What does your developer handoff look like?
My Figma files are hyper-organised. I deliver fully tokenised design systems with documented auto-layout components, variant states (hover/active/disabled), and structural redlining to ensure developers can build 1:1 without guesswork.
What is the typical turnaround time for a SaaS dashboard?
A full end-to-end dashboard design typically ranges from 4 to 8 weeks, depending on complexity. This includes discovery, wireframing, high-fidelity UI, and the creation of the component library.
Do you work with industries outside of Tech/SaaS?
Yes. While my core expertise is B2B SaaS, I have successfully applied product thinking to construction tech (Prime Estimate), logistics, and consumer e-commerce. The principles of UX remain universal.
