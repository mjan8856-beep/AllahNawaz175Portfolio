FAQ

Common Questions

What does your design process look like from start to finish?
Every project starts with a discovery and alignment session to understand your business goals, users, and constraints. From there I move through research, information architecture, wireframing, high-fidelity UI, prototyping, and usability testing before a clean developer handoff. The full 16-step framework is outlined in the Process section above.
How long does a typical project take?
A UX Audit typically takes 1–2 weeks. A full Product Redesign ranges from 4–8 weeks depending on scope and the number of revision rounds. Retainer partnerships are ongoing and structured around 2-week sprint cycles.
Do you work with international clients?
Absolutely. I'm based in Islamabad, Pakistan and work with clients globally across the US, UK, Europe, and beyond. All collaboration happens remotely via Figma, Slack, and video calls — timezone-friendly scheduling is always available.
What tools do you use for design and handoff?
My primary tool is Figma for all UI design, prototyping, and design systems. I use Framer for high-fidelity motion prototypes, Spline 3D for 3D elements, Maze and UserTesting for research, and Adobe Creative Suite for graphic and brand work. Developer handoffs are delivered with full Figma Inspect documentation and design tokens.
What makes you different from other designers?
I operate at the intersection of product strategy and visual craft. I don't just deliver beautiful screens — I think about business outcomes, user psychology, and engineering constraints simultaneously. My work is consistently data-informed, and I communicate decisions clearly so teams can move faster. Clients often describe me as someone who makes the complex feel simple.
